About

Frenly Dogs is an emotive 100-item pixel-art set which depicts our beloved Doge in various getups and backdrops. Mint cost is 6905 DOG, and you must be whitelisted to participate. Get your whitelist spot at the Doge NFT Layer3 page.

Rarities and Traits

  • Cigarette: 15%
  • Collar: 10%
  • Special Background: 4%
  • Special Item: 3%

FAQ

  • No. If you choose to sell your Frenly Dog, you will keep 100% of the proceeds!

  • No-- Frenly Dogs are only available for $DOG holders. If you don't have any, but would still like to buy an FND, you must first buy $DOG. We apologize for any inconvenience!
  • Will there ever be more than 100 pieces minted? What does the future look like?
  • This is it. Future development will seek to add value to the original 100, and integrate them into other Doge NFT projects.
  • Are FND's copyrighted? / Are there restrictions on how I can use my dog?
  • You are free to use your dog in any fashion you please. It's yours for life! Take care of it :)
